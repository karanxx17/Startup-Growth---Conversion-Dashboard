# Executive Summary
## Startup Growth & Conversion Dashboard — February 2020

---

### Project Overview
This project analyzes **173,198 real e-commerce behavioral events** from a multi-category online store to build a PM-grade startup metrics dashboard. The dataset covers user journeys from product views through cart additions to completed purchases during February 2020.

---

### North Star Metric: 5.13% Overall Conversion
Only 5.13% of users who viewed a product completed a purchase. **94.87% of traffic is not converting.** This is the single number every initiative must improve.

---

### Key Metrics At a Glance

| Metric | Value | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Daily Active Users (DAU) | 37,287 | Baseline |
| Total Revenue | $697,551 | Healthy |
| Total Purchases | 2,418 | Low |
| Avg Order Value | $288.48 | Strong |
| View → Cart % | 14.85% | **Critical gap** |
| Cart → Purchase % | 34.55% | Above avg (industry ~25%) |
| Overall Conversion % | **5.13%** | **North Star** |
| Repeat Buyer Rate | 17.85% | Needs improvement |

---

### What Is Going Wrong?

**Problem 1 (Critical): 85.1% drop-off between view and cart**
31,424 users viewed products but never added to cart. This is the single largest revenue leak.

**Problem 2 (High): 65.5% cart abandonment**
3,588 users carted but did not purchase. Likely caused by surprise shipping costs or checkout friction.

**Problem 3 (Medium): 82.2% one-time buyer rate**
Only 339 of 1,894 buyers returned for a second purchase. No retention strategy exists.

---

### Top Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| P0 | A/B test product page CTAs, add reviews | +3-5pp view-to-cart |
| P1 | Cart abandonment email (1hr/24hr/72hr) | Recover 15-20% of abandoned carts |
| P1 | Simplify checkout, show shipping upfront | -10pp abandonment |
| P2 | Post-purchase loyalty program | Repeat rate 17.9% → 25%+ |
| P3 | Diversify beyond construction tools | Reduce 68% revenue concentration |

---

### Revenue Risk Alert
Construction tools alone account for **68.2% ($476K) of total revenue**. This single-category dependency is a critical business risk that must be addressed through category diversification.
